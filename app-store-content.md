# App Store Connect İçeriği

## Description (Açıklama)
Sunbusters finds sunny and partly cloudy cities near your location. Just tap the button, and discover places with clear skies or gentle clouds wherever you are.

Features:
- Find sunny cities near your current location
- Switch between "Only Sunny" and "Partly Cloudy" filters
- View distance to each city
- Tap a city to open its location in Google Maps
- Works worldwide with OpenWeatherMap data

Perfect for travelers, photographers, and anyone who wants to chase the sun.
